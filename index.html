About me

I am a web designer and developer geek with over 2 years experience. Nevertheless, my line of work has given me the opportunity to learn about other areas of design like branding and printed media.

My love for sleek, simple and comprehensive sites grew fonder through my education, and is reflected in every project I work on.

When I am not obsessing over a project I am usually at a local record store buying some vinyl or going around the world seeking rare and unique watches.
